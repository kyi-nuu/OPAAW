2.

Education

Overview

The aim here is to give women and girls equal access to good education at all levels. It tackles the barriers they face, like financial issues and societal stereotypes, and promotes vocational training and gender-sensitive teaching. The objective is to ensure that women and girls get the skills they need for decision-making and career growth, while also changing outdated views about their education

Sub-Objectives

Ensuring equal access for women and girls to education and training to equip them with necessary knowledge and skills to participate in decision-making processes.

Promoting the education of women and girls through the adoption of necessary policies and programs.

Facilitating free, compulsory and equal access for boys and girls to quality preschool, primary and secondary education.

Promoting specially designed vocational and technical courses for women in non-traditional and various fields.

Building capacity of teachers, at all levels, regarding the principles of equality and non-discrimination through providing gender-sensitive training materials

Working on correcting prevailing misperceptions and behaviours of parents through holding training sessions and activities.

Ensuring that women and girls have equal access to career development, training, scholarships and fellowships.

Promoting increased access for women to tertiary education.

Ensuring that curriculum and course materials at all levels are gender-sensitive.

Quoted Objective: Education: providing equal opportunity for all women and girls to have access to quality education at all levels of vocational and skills training, as well as literacy programmes.
