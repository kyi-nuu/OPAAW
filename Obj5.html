5.

Social Protection

Overview

This objective is all about making sure women’s social needs, safety, and well-being are met. It focuses on changing societal attitudes, improving national policies, and working with civil society to support women, especially those who are disadvantaged. It also emphasizes the importance of family unity and non-discrimination within the family.

Sub-Objectives

Providing legal protection for women against all forms of violence and discrimination.

Establishing support services for women who are victims of violence and discrimination.

Raising public awareness on the importance of protecting women from violence and discrimination.

Promoting the role of women in society and their contribution to the development of their communities and families by taking the necessary institutional steps.

Quoted Objective: Social protection: improving and ensuring women’s social needs, safety, and well-being.
