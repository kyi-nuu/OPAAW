About OPAAW

The Organisation of Islamic Cooperation's (OIC) Plan of Action for the Advancement of Women (OPAAW), adopted in 2008, is aimed at improving the status of women in its 57 member countries by advancing their rights and opportunities. This all-encompassing plan addresses key areas of women’s life outcomes, such as education, healthcare, economic participation, and legal protection.

Overall

OPAAW provides a framework and agreed-upon standard for OIC member countries to protect and improve the status of women where necessary. Crucially, it is entirely consistent with Islamic values and Shariah, as well as aligning with international standards. Member states are encouraged to develop national strategies based on this framework and report their progress regularly.
