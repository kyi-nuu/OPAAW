Economic Empowerment

Overview

The goal here is to boost women's access to economic opportunities in both public and private sectors. It includes efforts to eliminate poverty, match education with job market needs, and ensure equal pay for equal work. The objective also stresses the importance of family-friendly policies and creating a work environment free from discrimination, along with legal reforms to support women's financial independence.

Sub-Objectives
