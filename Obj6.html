6.

Protection of Women from Violence

Overview

This one is about fighting all forms of violence against women and girls, including human trafficking and harmful traditional practices. It calls for preventive measures, support for victims, and punishment for perpetrators. It also stresses the need for national policies and international cooperation to protect women from violence.

Sub-Objectives

Implementing legal reforms to protect women from violence.

Establishing support services for women who are victims of violence.

Raising public awareness on the importance of protecting women from violence.

Promoting the role of women in society and their contribution to the development of their communities and families by taking the necessary institutional steps

Quoted Objective: Protection of Women from Violence: Combating all forms of gender-based violence, human trafficking and other harmful traditional practices against women and girls. Combating different forms of violence against women and girls including deprivation of opportunities and full enjoyment of their rights through preventive measures and provisions of rehabilitation to victims and punishment of perpetrators.
