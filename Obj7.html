7.

Women in Crisis Situations

Overview

This objective ensures that women and girls get the protection and humanitarian aid they need during conflicts, natural disasters, and other crises. It highlights the importance of involving women in conflict resolution and peacebuilding. The goal is to make sure women are safe and have access to essential services during tough times.

Sub-Objectives

Ensuring gender-based response to women and girl refugees, migrants, displaced by armed conflict and foreign occupation, and facilitating their access to humanitarian safe zones, food shelter, education and healthcare in times of crisis.

Taking special measures to protect women and girls from different forms of gender-based violence and sexual abuse, in armed conflict and post-conflict situations, providing necessary assistance to victims and fighting the impunity of perpetrators.

Supporting the role of women and women's civil society organizations in the promotion of peaceful and inclusive societies through reconciliation, interfaith and intercultural dialogues among parties of the conflict.

Promoting the international cooperation in combating the violence against women and girls in armed conflicts.
